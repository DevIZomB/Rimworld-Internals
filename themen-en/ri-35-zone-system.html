Zone_Growing & Plant Growth

Growing zones, growth rate and temperature dependency

Temperature Connection
GenTemperature.TwelfthsInAverageTemperatureRange determines the growing season. Temperature is one of 5 growth rate factors.
#1 Zone_Growing RimWorld/Zone_Growing.cs

Implements IPlantToGrowSettable. Manages: plantDefToGrow (ThingDef), allowSow, allowCut flags. Default plant: Potato (or Toxipotato on pollution worlds).

GrowingQuadrumsDescription uses GenTemperature.TwelfthsInAverageTemperatureRange to display the available growing months to the player.

SetPlantDefToGrow is public and Harmony-patchable.

#2 Growth Rate (5-Factor Multiplication) RimWorld/Plant.cs

GrowthRate = Fertility × Temperature × Light × NoxiousHaze × Drought

FactorSourceNote
FertilityTerrain + Building0.0 - 1.4+
TemperatureRoom/Outdoor TempCurve: 0°C=0%, 10°C=ramp, 42°C=0%
LightGlowGrid0.0 (dark) - 1.0 (full)
NoxiousHazeGameConditionReduced during smog
DroughtGameConditionReduced during drought

Formula: GrowthPerTick = 1 / (60000 × growDays) × GrowthRate

#3 Life Stages

Sowing (<0.01%) → Growing (0.01%-99.9%) → Mature (>99.9%)

Updated every 2000 ticks (TickLong). Growth is added per TickLong.

#4 Death Mechanics (5 Types) RimWorld/Plant.cs

• Lifespan — Maximum lifespan reached
• Light deprivation — Too little light over time
• Exposure — Temperature outside growth range
• Vacuum — No roof + extreme climate
• Pollution — Polluted cells

#5 Harvest & Blight RimWorld/Plant.cs

YieldNow() uses GenMath.RoundRandom for yield calculation. MP-Critical — consumes Rand!

Blight: FloodFill up to 100 adjacent cells. Spreads across growing zones.

MP-Critical
TickLong() generates leaf motes that consume global Rand state (NOT in PushState) — affects all plants with dropLeaves=true. Desync risk!
