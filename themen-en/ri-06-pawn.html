RI-06 Namespace: Verse

Pawn

Most complex class in RimWorld — ~45 trackers, ~100+ properties

#1 ~45 Tracker Fields Verse/Pawn.cs:16-134

Every pawn has ~45 tracker fields (health, jobs, needs, mindState, stances, skills, story, relations, etc.). Each tracker is its own object with its own tick cycle.

#2 Pawn.Tick (Order) Verse/Pawn.cs:1525-1603

MP-Critical — ~20 trackers are ticked in fixed order: stances → pather → jobs → health → mindState → needs → etc. Order is gameplay-relevant.

#3 Pawn.TickInterval (Camera-Dependent) Verse/Pawn.cs:1605-1716

MP-Critical — Delta-based ticking. Interval depends on camera distance (closer = more frequent). In multiplayer this must be synchronized since different players have different camera positions.

#5 SpawnSetup Verse/Pawn.cs:1345-1455

Spawn logic: Dead pawns are converted to corpse, WorldPawns list is updated, PawnComponentsUtility initializes all trackers that are still null.

#6 Kill (6 Phases) Verse/Pawn.cs:2075-2326

MP-Critical — 250 lines, 6 phases: PreKill hooks → Health state → Corpse spawn → Relations notify → WorldPawns → PostKill. Rand for corpse rot variation.

#7 ~100+ Properties Verse/Pawn.cs:213-800

Over 100 properties for quick access: IsColonist, IsPrisoner, IsSlave, Faction, Position, Drafted, Downed, Dead, InMentalState, etc.

#8 Temperature Connection

ComfortableTemperatureRange = pawn stat ± 10°C buffer (SafeTemperatureRange). Outside of that → Hypothermia/Heatstroke hediffs. Apparel stats modify the range. Genes can also shift it.

Connection: → RI-14 (Health/Damage), → RI-22 (Need_Outdoors/Indoors)
