#6 Temperature Hediffs RimWorld/HediffGiver_Heat.cs, RimWorld/HediffGiver_Hypothermia.cs

Automatic hediff assignment based on room temperature vs. SafeTemperatureRange:

ConditionHediffMechanism
Temp > SafeMaxHeatstrokeSeverity increases proportional to overshoot
Temp < SafeMinHypothermiaSeverity increases proportional to undershoot

SafeTemperatureRange = ComfortableTemperatureRange ± 10°C. Apparel (parka, duster) extends the range. Genes can also shift it.

Connection: → Temperature System (v2 main page), → RI-06 (Pawn)

#7 Rot Rate (Decay) Verse/GenTemperature.cs

Temperature-dependent decay: 0°C → 0% decay, 10°C → 100% decay. Linear. Below 0°C = frozen = no decay. Coolers (<10°C) slow down, freezers (<0°C) completely stop decay.
