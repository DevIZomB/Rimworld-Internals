RI-22 Namespace: RimWorld

Need_Outdoors & Need_Indoors

Fresh air and shelter needs — position-based delta calculation

Temperature Connection
Both needs depend on UsesOutdoorTemperature and RoofDef — the same properties that control the room temperature system.
#1 Need_Outdoors RimWorld/Need_Outdoors.cs:1-144

Fresh air need. Delta is calculated purely based on position — no Rand involved.

PositionDelta per Tick
Outdoor + NoRoof+8.0
Outdoor + ThinRoof+5.0 (estimated, reduced)
Indoor + NoRoof+3.0
Indoor + ThinRoof (minimum: 0.2)-0.32
Indoor + ThickRoof-0.45

InBed modifier: When delta is negative and pawn is in bed → ×0.2 (slower decay while sleeping).

Patchable via Harmony. Delta values are fields, not constants.

#2 6 Severity Categories

Free (100-80%) → Satisfied (80-60%) → Want (60-40%) → Need (40-20%) → CabinFeverSevere (20-5%) → Entombed (5-0%)

Low values cause MentalBreak risk (Wandering, Tantrum).

#3 Need_Indoors RimWorld/Need_Indoors.cs:1-109

Opposite of Need_Outdoors for pawns with PrefersIndoors trait. Increases when indoor with ThickRoof/ThinRoof, decreases when outdoors. Mutually exclusive — a pawn has either Need_Outdoors OR Need_Indoors, never both.

Disabled when PrefersOutdoors is active (and vice versa).

#4 Connection to Room System

Key properties:
• Room.UsesOutdoorTemperature — ≥25% no roof OR TouchesMapEdge
• Room.PsychologicallyOutdoors — ≥300 open roof cells OR (MapEdge + ≥50% open)
• RoofDef (NoRoof / ThinRoof / ThickRoof) determines the specific delta value
• GlowGrid / PsychGlow additionally affects both needs

Cross-reference: RI-21 (Room/Temperature), RI-06 (Pawn), RI-14 (Health)

MP Note
Need_Outdoors/Indoors themselves consume no Rand. However: Need_Rest uses Rand.MTBEventOccurs and Need_Food uses Rand.ValueSeeded — other needs in the same NeedsTracker are MP-critical!
